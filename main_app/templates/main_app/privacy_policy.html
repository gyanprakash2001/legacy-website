Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 16, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the use of services provided by Legacy Website Integration (the "App").

Information We Collect

The App collects limited personal information solely necessary for the core functionality of integrating your social media content:

  • **Social Platform Data (Instagram/Facebook):** We collect your unique **User ID** and **Access Token**. This data is required to read and display your own public and private media (photos, videos) on your profile.
  • **Contact Information:** We collect the email address provided during user registration.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity on Instagram and Facebook.
  • To retrieve your own authorized media and display it on your website profile/dashboard.
  • To maintain secure, long-term access to your social media data via the Long-Lived Access Token.

Data Sharing

We **do not** share, sell, or rent your Instagram User ID, Access Token, or any derived media content with any third parties or external advertisers.

User Rights and Contact

You may revoke the App's access to your Instagram and Facebook account at any time via your settings on Instagram/Facebook. Upon account deletion from our service, all stored access tokens will be immediately destroyed.
